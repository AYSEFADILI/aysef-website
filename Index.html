Our Story

Founded by Dadi Masesa, AYSEF was born from a simple realization: the "Education Gap" in Kenya means children from poor backgrounds receive five fewer years of effective schooling than their peers. Talent alone isn't enough to bridge that gap. AYSEF exists to be the bridge.

The Dual-Structure Model

Adili Sports Academy: Our high-performance technical arm that develops elite talent.

AYSEF Foundation: Our social impact engine that manages scholarships, welfare, and community development.

The Academy

The Residential Hub

Location: Kisumu (Riat Hills / Mamboleo)

Concept: A full-time boarding academy where nutrition, recovery, academics, and training are synchronized.

Facilities: Future home of the Adili Multi-Sport Complex featuring FIBA courts, a High-Performance Gym, and Recovery Center.

The "Scholar-Athlete" Curriculum

We create a "360-Degree Athlete":

  • Core Academics: Rigorous homeschooling/tutorial support for KCSE/IGCSE.
  • STEM & Tech: Mandatory "Tech Tuesday" coding and data analysis workshops to prepare for the digital economy.
  • Financial Literacy: Teaching personal finance and contract law from age 14.

Career Tracks

Medicine: Mentorships with sports physiotherapists and doctors.

Technology: Data science applied to sports performance.

Sports Law: Understanding contracts, agency, and rights.
